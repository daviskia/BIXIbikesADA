Our project on data analysis for social good focuses on the bikeshare system in Montreal and how we can improve the system using data analysis in order to encourage bike travel as a mode of transport. Bikes are both more environmentally friendly and healthy than other modes of transport such as cars and buses. By adapting the bikeshare system to better suit the needs of the users we can encourage bike travel and improve citizens' tranport experiences which are such an integral part of our everyday lives.
